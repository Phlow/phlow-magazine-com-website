Rowolo’s Creative Commons Music Netlabel Moments 2009

Konrad is the head behind rowolo.de on of the best netlabelsnews and netlabellist-websites on the web. But like a real player, that's not enough and he has to spread a little more of that love for music on his Cuts&Mouse Radio Show. And if that's still not enough for the radio lovers, he contributes his monthly CC-Music Mouse Mix to notheen.com, audioasyl.net and coloradio.org.

On the other hands he's a selekta for phonocake.org a netlabel for electronic music from Dresden/Germany and piepmatz-records.de a netlabel for home recording pop music.

It's a pleasure to present his charts! Roll'em in!
