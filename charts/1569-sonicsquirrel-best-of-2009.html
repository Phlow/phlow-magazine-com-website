Sonicsquirrel’s Creative Commons Music Netlabel Moments 2009

Sonicsquirrel is the swiss music hub for netaudio-, netlabel- and creative commons music culture. Like archive.org they help netlabels to build communities, fanbases by providing the netaudio scene with the basics: a storage for their music. Please give the people a big applause for their commitment and helping netlabels to grow, to build upon and to spread their love for music.

We're proud to present the charts of the sonicsquirrel team on Phlow, but you can dig them of course on their own beautiful new-designed territory under www.sonicsquirrel.net/special/phlow_2009.
