Rute Correia’s Creative Commons Music Netlabel Moments 2009

Marca Branca (in english “White Label”) is a portuguese netaudio radio show and podcast, produced and hosted by Rute Correia. It airs weekly on Rádio Zero in Portugal and Radio Filispim 93.9FM (Galicia, Spain). At first, the idea was just to discover new sounds on the web, wandering around netlabels. But this galaxy was far too large to be condensed as only netlabels records: soon it became a small window to an endless universe. The last editions include special features on themes such as The Wired CD or Jamendo Pro. And since variety is one of the key-words of the show, you can almost play "the odd-one out" with this. Take a listen and have fun!
