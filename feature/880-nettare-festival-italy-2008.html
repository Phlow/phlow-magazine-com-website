Nettare: First Festival of the Italian Netaudio Movement

Italian Netaudio Festival // In italy the netaudio movement is heading for the next step: The Nettare Festival. Nettare is meant to be the starting point of a web structured development of Netaudio in Italy. The website shall be a place to share experiences and to promote musicians. That’s why 27 Italian netlabels joined forces and reach out for the Italian public on 6th december with a musical and theoretical program. The whole festival is connecting four cities in Italy: Bologna, Torino, Genova and Firenze (click on each town to have a look on the map).

Phlow-Magazine.com is proud to be a media partner and we’ll try to interview the busy guys soon! Till then: Let’s have a look on the program!
