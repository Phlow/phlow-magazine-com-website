Phlow Mag Exclusive: Boring Music Vol. 6

Boring Music // With some delay, I finally managed to publish my new Netaudio mixtape, the sixth installation of the BORING MUSIC series I started four years back. Please enjoy this 70 minutes trip via 15 tracks from 15 releases I exclusively reviewed for PHLOW MAGAZINE!

Boring Music Vol. 6 Mixtape (MP3)

As always, the mix comes off quite versatile. Starting with Indierock, I’m moving to experimental Ambient, Downbeat, and end up with some square-edged Techno tracks. Enjoy, and download the free EPs that stand behind the single songs I chose for the mix… Please spread.
